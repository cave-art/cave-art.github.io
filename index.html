REHARMONIZER MIDI KEYBOARD
ABOUT
The Reharmonizer is a MIDI keyboard that can play layers of harmonies based on the notes you play and the key you’re in, offering a new approach to melodic and chordal composition.

Features:
  • Monophonic
  • Key selection knob
  • Major/minor mode selection
  • Octave shifting
  • Harmonic spread alteration features, with the option to randomize
  • A velocity knob that goes up to 11
  • USB port for connectivity with any DAW
  • Available in black, silver, blue, green, red, and yellow
Assembly and testing is done by hand in Toronto, Ontario. Due to inventory costs, this product is made upon order. Please allow for up to 2 months for your order to be manufactured and shipped. I will send frequent updates and if at any point you have any additional concerns, please send an email to me@sava.solar.
